Seecure

Android application development

Developed and maintained Android application. From scratch, developed a safety and security app. Working with Google Maps API sharing geographical location with friends and colleagues. Manage location sharing between groups and contacts. The main feature of the app was the alarm. When pulling the alarm, you will send a distress signal to people near you with the app, and contacts you are sharing your location with. At the same time, the phone makes alot of noise and blinks.
Authentication and verification of users through Firebase Authentication API.








Design of security application

Through continuous work and numerous iterations structured the design of security application. Design was based on Material Design and Android design guidelines provided by Google. Worked with chief who was very demanding on the design and wanted to create something simple and new, but which contained complex functionalities.
